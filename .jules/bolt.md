## 2025-02-14 - [Double-evaluation of getAvailablePlayers in RoundPlanner]
**Learning:** Found a specific bottleneck in `RoundPlanner.jsx` where the expensive list derivation logic in `getAvailablePlayers` (which filters and sorts a potentially large array of players) was being called multiple times inside the render loop for the picker modal (once for `map` and once for `.length === 0`). Since this happened on every render, it significantly slowed down typing in the search bar and selecting players.
**Action:** Use `useMemo` to cache expensive derived state like `availablePlayers`, `playerTeamMap`, and `duplicateIds`, rather than evaluating them via inline functions during every render, especially when the resulting arrays are iterated over multiple times or when typing causes frequent re-renders.
