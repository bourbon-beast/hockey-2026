## 2024-05-18 - Icon-Only Button Accessibility in TeamColumn
**Learning:** Found multiple icon-only buttons (Remove player, Sort mode toggle) in `TeamColumn.jsx` lacking `aria-label` attributes and missing keyboard focus states. The inner `<svg>` elements also lacked `aria-hidden="true"`, causing redundant screen reader announcements. Interactive buttons shown only on hover (`group-hover:opacity-100`) also need `focus-visible:opacity-100` to ensure they are visible when navigating by keyboard.
**Action:** When adding icon-only buttons, always include descriptive `aria-label`s, add `aria-hidden="true"` to inner SVGs, ensure proper keyboard styling with `focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:outline-none focus-visible:ring-blue-400 p-0.5 rounded`, and make hover-only actions focusable via `focus-visible:opacity-100`.
