## 2024-04-27 - Icon-Only Button Accessibility in Lists and Modals
**Learning:** Found a specific pattern in the squad tracker where icon-only action buttons (like move up/down, remove player, toggle confirmation, and modal close) completely lack context for screen reader users and fail to display clear focus indicators for keyboard navigation. Additionally, inner elements like SVGs or text symbols (like '×') can cause redundant or confusing announcements if not hidden.
**Action:** Always verify that icon-only buttons include descriptive `aria-label`s, that their inner graphical content is marked with `aria-hidden="true"`, and that they have explicitly defined `focus-visible` styles with adequate padding (e.g., `focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:outline-none p-0.5 rounded`) to ensure the focus ring does not appear uncomfortably tight around the icon.
